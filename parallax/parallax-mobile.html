Why North of Portugal

North of Portugal is a safe zone, ideal for Remote WORK has several places to visit in your spare time, such as exploration of the Douro region, Natural Reserves (Gêres), several regions with beaches and rivers, fresh air with plenty of nature. Area also rich in monuments such as Sanctuaries, Castles and Churches.
